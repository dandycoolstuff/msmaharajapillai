M.S.Maharaja pillai was born on July 30, 1918 in Munanjipatti, Tirunelveli district, Tamil Nadu to Shanmuagam pillai and Ulagammal. He had two sisters namely Karapaga Sundarathammal and Gomathiammal.

In 1935 he married Kamala gandhimathi daughter of R.M.Gopalakrishna pillai from Sattankulam. Kamala gandhimathi and Maharaja pillai had 3 daughters and 5 sons.
