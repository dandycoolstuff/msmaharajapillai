M.S.Maharaja Pillai

(1918-1971)

M.S.Maharaja pillai who was  the municipal chairman of Palayamkottai for 15 years continuously from 1952 to 1967. He served the people of Palayamkottai reluctantly and initiated so many projects and processes long time back for Tirunelveli to become a corporation today. He dedicated his life for the people of Palayamkottai. The town Maharaja nagar near palayamkottai was named after him. A statue of Maharaja pillai was opened in Maharaja nagar  in 2016 to honor his services to the people of Palayamkottai.
